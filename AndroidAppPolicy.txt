Whey Protein Calculator: Privacy policy

Welcome to the Protein Price Calculator app for Android!

This app is used to find out if the protein price is reasonable or not. I am always on the hunt for products that has the best price to protein ratio. :)

The data entered on the app isn't transmitted elsewhere and stays only within the app.

No personally identifiable information is transmitted elsewhere. All data entered is used by your device only.

If you find any security vulnerability that has been inadvertently caused by me, or have any question regarding how the app protects your privacy, please send me an email.
