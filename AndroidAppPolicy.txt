Whey Protein Calculator: Privacy policy

Welcome to the Protein Price Calculator app for Android!

This is an app is used to find out if the protein price is reasonable or not.

The data entered on the app isn't transmitted elsewhere and stays only within the app.

No personally identifiable information is transmitted elsewhere. All data entered is used by your device only.

If you find any security vulnerability that has been inadvertently caused by me, or have any question regarding how the app protecte your privacy, please send me an email and I will surely try to fix it/help you.
